Who We Are

Fostering People's Education, Empowerment and Enterprise, Inc. (FosPEEE)

FosPEEE is a non-profit, non-stock, non-government organization that aims to promote community empowerment for environmental stewardship, restoration, and conservation of biodiversity, and sustainable development in key conservation sites in the Philippines.

To implement, advocate, and scale the Rainforestation farming system in degraded landscapes, creating self-sustaining Legacy Forests in partnership with local communities, government, and the private sector.

Mission

Vision

A Philippines where thriving, biodiverse forest ecosystems are valued and sustainably managed by empowered communities, contributing to national resilience against climate change.

Our Purpose

To promote community empowerment for environmental stewardship, restoration and conservation of biodiversity, and sustainable development.

To foster the education, empowerment, and social enterprise of people and communities that encourage biodiversity conservation and sustainable development.

To promote, develop, implement, engage in, and/or support biodiversity-friendly social enterprises.

To collaborate and partner with other civil society organizations, local government units, national government agencies, academe, and other stakeholders to promote environmental stewardship, restoration and conservation of biodiversity, and sustainable development.

To generate resources through fundraising to pursue environmental stewardship, restoration and conservation of biodiversity, and sustainable development.
